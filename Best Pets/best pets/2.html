Dogs have a monologue

  Before I take me home, remember that my life span is about ten to fifteen years. If you leave me, it will be my greatest pain; Please don't be angry with me for long, and don't lock me up as punishment. Do you understand? You have your job, your entertainment, your friends, and you are my only one; Always speak to me, even if I don't understand what you say, but I will feel your voice with me; When I want to live the most arduous journey of life and death, please do not say: "I can't bear to see. I don't want to be there." ;
